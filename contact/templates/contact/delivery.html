Delivery and Returns

Delivery

I currently only deliver to the UK, all delivery costs are free for customers. COVID UPDATE : Due to COVID 19 related restrictions some orders are experiencing delays in the postal service. Please be patient and rest assured your parcel will arrive as soon as possible. All artworks are dispatched within 3-5 working days. Orders are sent via 1st class signed for delivery.

Returns

I hope you love your original art piece or print as much as I do, but in the unlikely event that you are not satisfied with your artwork(s), you can cancel your purchase and receive a full refund (minus P&P charges). To qualify for a refund, the print(s) must be sent back to Rachel Clayton in their original condition and protective packaging within 14 days of receiving your order. These terms and conditions do not affect your statutory rights as a consumer.

To arrange a refund, simply go to our contact page and send a message labelled 'Return', within 14 days and we can arrange an exchange or return.
